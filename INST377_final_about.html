Defining the Information Problem

With the heavy reliance on teamwork in Valorant, it can be difficult to figure out what agents to play as when loading into a match. Each map has it's own individual quirks and this can affect agent usability across each one. This is especially heightened for beginner players as favorable agent choices tend tend to not be that important and most rely on their pure aiming skill. As you reach the middle and higher ranks however, teamwork becomes a bigger emphasis and players can often get stuck in certain ranks because they donâ€™t know how to optimize their agent selection for certain maps. The variety of roles also lead to even more choice overload.

Intended Use Case

This website acts as a beginner guide to competitive Valorant. There brief information available such as competitive ranks, the top 3 weapons in the game, a list of all the agents in the game, as well as the maps that are available. Proper agent selection and proper role distribution is key to team success as they heavily impact how coordinated your team is and this is the use case that this website aims to serve most. By simply searching a map through the agent matcher page, one can view the most ideal agents to select on a map basis based on win percentage.
